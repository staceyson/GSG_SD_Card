
Super NES | Super Nintendo

File Extension: .sfc
	Format: .sfc

    BIOS Files: Not required

Face buttons are the same layout as a Super Nintendo controller (A and B are switched, X and Y are switched)
L1 and R1 are the shoulder buttons
Keypad button 1 sets a save state
Keypad button 3 loads the save state
Keypad button 5 takes a screenshot
Credit button resets the cart
